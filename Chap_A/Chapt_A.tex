\chapter{Amplitude and Phase Error Tables}
\label{app:amp_phase_errors}

All errors are computed relative to the reference case 1\_ConstantAmplitude.
The columns report the percentage error in lift force amplitude,
lift force phase, moment amplitude, and moment phase at each reduced velocity.
